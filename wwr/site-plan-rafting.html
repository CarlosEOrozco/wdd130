Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our water rafting company is to provide thrilling and safe water rafting experiences that connect people with the great outdoors. We aim to offer high-quality services that cater to all levels of rafters, from beginners to experienced adventurers. Our goal is to promote the sport of water rafting, inspire a love for outdoor adventure, and create unforgettable experiences on the water

Audience

Our target audience is primarily adults aged 18-50 who enjoy outdoor activities and are looking for a new adventure. We also welcome families with children above the age of 12, ensuring that safety measures are in place for younger rafters.

Scenario Questions:

Question: I’m a beginner and have never been water rafting before. Is it safe for me to book a trip?
Answer: Absolutely! We have experienced guides who will ensure your safety and provide a thorough briefing before the trip. We also provide all necessary safety equipment.

Question: I’m an experienced rafter. Do you offer challenging routes?
Answer: Yes, we do. We have a variety of routes that cater to different skill levels, including challenging ones for experienced rafters.

Question: I want to book a family trip but I have a child who is 10 years old. Can they participate?
Answer: For safety reasons, we only allow children above the age of 12 to participate in our rafting trips. However, we offer other family-friendly activities that your younger child might enjoy."
