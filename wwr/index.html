The River Rush

Experience the thrill of the rapids on this full-day adventure down the River Rush. This trip is perfect for adrenaline junkies, featuring class IV and V rapids, stunning waterfalls, and a riverside lunch.

Calm Waters

Ideal for families and those new to rafting, the Calm Waters trip offers a gentle introduction to the sport. Enjoy a leisurely paddle down the serene river, with plenty of opportunities to swim, wildlife watch, and soak up the sun.

Moonlight Voyage

Our Moonlight Voyage is a unique night-time trip that lets you experience the river under the stars. Navigate the gentle rapids with the help of our expert guides and enjoy a riverside campfire dinner. This trip offers a unique perspective of the wilderness and a chance to see nocturnal wildlife.
